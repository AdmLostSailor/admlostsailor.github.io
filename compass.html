The Terrapin Compass

Every song, plotted by heading and distance. The heading is the movement it belongs to — nine bearings around the dial, in suite order. The distance from the hub is its directionality: how near the song sits to Terrapin, and whether its needle points home or away.

How to Read the Dial

Within — already home, dwelling near the hub
Toward — needle tip drawn in toward Terrapin
Away — needle tip driven out toward the rim
Ambiguous — hovering mid-distance, undecided
Instrumental — a wordless marker, no vector

The nine spokes are the movements of the suite, in order clockwise from the top. Click any needle to open the song's full profile.
